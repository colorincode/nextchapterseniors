Types of Senior Living Supported

Whether you're seeking independent or assisted living, memory care, respite (short term) or a Board and Care Home in the Bay Area, I'll help you find a community that matches your or your loved one's needs and desires.


Independent Living

Perfect for active seniors who want a vibrant, social community with resort-style amenities like fitness centers, dining, and activities. Many independent living communities offer flexibilty if needs change.

Board & Care

Ideal for seniors seeking a cozy, home-like setting, Silicon Valley board and care homes provide personalized support, nutritious meals, and engaging activities tailored to individual needs.

Respite Care

Tailored for seniors needing short-term care or caregivers seeking a break, respite care communities offer temporary stays with personalized support, engaging activities, and a safe, welcoming environment.

Memory Care

Designed for seniors with Alzheimer's or dementia, these communities provide specialized care in safe, supportive settings. Staff are trained to foster engagement and quality of life through personalized plans.

Assisted Living

Ideal for seniors needing help with daily tasks like meals or medication management, while staying engaged in a warm, social environment. These communities offer private apartments, dining, and tailored support.
